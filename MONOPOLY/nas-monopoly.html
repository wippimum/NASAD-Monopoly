Monopoly: NAS Abu Dhabi Edition

Designed by Alya, Leyna, Sultan and Hamdan AL FALASI

How to Play

NAS Abu Dhabi Edition — a school-themed Monopoly for 2–6 players.

🎯 The Goal

Be the last player left with money! Travel around the school, buy your favourite spots, charge rent when friends land on them, and try not to go bankrupt. When only one player still has money, that player wins.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Setup

  • Add 2 to 6 players. Each picks a name and a token.
  • Everyone starts with 1,500 ⭐.
  • All players begin on GO and take turns.

🎲 Your Turn

  • Tap Roll Dice and your token moves that many tiles around the board.
  • Roll doubles (both dice the same) and you get to roll again!
  • But roll doubles three times in a row and you’re sent straight to the Principal’s Office. 😬
  • Each time you pass or land on GO, collect 200 ⭐.

🏫 Buying Property

  • Land on an unowned space (a classroom, library, gym, gate, etc.) and you may buy it for the price shown on the tile.
  • If you don’t buy it, it simply stays unowned.
  • Own a space and other players must pay you rent when they land on it.

💰 Rent & Building

  • Collect every space of one colour zone to get a monopoly — rent doubles!
  • With a monopoly you can build houses 🏠 (and then a hotel 🏨) on those spaces to charge much higher rent.
  • You must build evenly across the zone — one house on each space before adding a second.
  • More houses = more rent: 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 houses, then a hotel charges the most.

🚪 Gates & Utilities

  • Gates (stations): rent depends on how many gates you own — 1 gate = 25 ⭐, 2 = 50 ⭐, 3 = 100 ⭐, 4 = 200 ⭐.
  • Reception & Water Station (utilities): rent is your dice roll × 4. Own both and it’s dice × 10.

🎁 Chance & 📱 Toddle Cards

  • Land on a Chance space to draw a card — usually a happy surprise that gives you ⭐.
  • Land on a Toddle Card space and you might have to pay a small fine (forgot your PE kit again!).
  • Do exactly what the card says.

🎓 Special Corners

  • GO — collect 200 ⭐ each time around.
  • Principal’s Office (jail) — when you’re sent here you’re stuck. Pay 50 ⭐ to leave, or roll doubles to escape (up to 3 turns).
  • Go to Principal — land here and you’re sent straight to the Principal’s Office.
  • Break Time — a free rest! Nothing happens. 😌
  • School Fees — pay 200 ⭐ when you land here.

🏆 Winning

If you can’t afford to pay what you owe, you go bankrupt and leave the game. The last player still in the game wins! You can also tap End Game at any time to rank everyone by total worth (cash + property + buildings).
